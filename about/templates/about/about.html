jo dow communication coaching

Jo Dow a Communications Coach specialising in Presentation and Authenticity.

He is one of a small number of coaches who have worked as part of RADA Business to deliver world class communication training to some of the most influential organisations in the world.

His intuitive approach is tailored to his clients exact needs. Responding to their challenges and goals with individual specific insights and clarity on how they deliver.

Communication Coach & Roleplay Facilitator

Jo trained at the Guildhall School of Music and Drama under Patsy Rodenburg OBE and after 25 years on TV, film and stage, playing regular charcacters in many iconic series, clocking up over 500 episodes from live studio sitcom to 13 million viewers, police drama, sci-fi, 75mm widescreen at sea, ground-breaking medical drama and writer-led TV films for writers like Linda La Plante, Jed Mercurio and Jimmy McGovern.

He has also trained communicators for the cabinet office, civil service, and many business organisations across the world.
