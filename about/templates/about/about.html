About

Jo Dow Communication Coaching

Jo Dow is a Communications Coach specializing in Presentation and Authenticity. He is one of a small number of coaches who have worked as part of RADA Business to deliver world-class communication training to some of the most influential organizations in the world.

His intuitive approach is tailored to his clients' exact needs, responding to their challenges and goals with individual-specific insights and clarity on how they deliver.

Jo Dow

Communication Coach & Roleplay Facilitator

Jo trained at the Guildhall School of Music and Drama under Patsy Rodenburg OBE and, after 25 years on TV, film, and stage, has been a regular character in many iconic series. He has appeared in over 500 episodes, ranging from live studio sitcoms to police dramas and groundbreaking medical dramas.

He has also trained communicators for the Cabinet Office, civil service, and many business organizations across the world.
